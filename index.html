THE BEGINNING OF SYMPOSIUM!

What is Symposium?

SYMPOSIUM, one of JIIC's major events, is here for young and creative minds brimming with ideas. A platform for young entrepreneurs or anyone with an idea to turn their vision into a reality. To convert knowledge into impact and to propel innovation. A location where anyone can display their ideas. The conference attracts top periodicals and publishing houses. And some of the best ideas are then published at a bare minimum cost in these prestigious publications, giving the candidate an edge in his start-up. Our platform brings together people from all over the world who feel their ideas are significant enough to share with others, as well as a supportive atmosphere. A panel of experts will provide you with the finest guidance for leveraging growth strategies as well as valuable insights on your ideas.

Advantages Of Participating

  • The major benefit Is that the published ideas cannot be replicated by others since they have already been published under your name. It serves as an IPR (Intellectual property rights).
  • Inspiring presentations from business executives to assist candidates in developing their ideas.
  • A supportive environment with like-minded people is offered to help you turn your ideas into reality.

What is JIIC?

Jaypee Innovation and Incubation Center (JIIC), is established at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida to cultivate innovative and entrepreneurial ecosystem by providing Incubator and accelerated services. JIIC is envisioned to bring forth entrepreneurial thoughts and knowledge by creating opportunity for Students and Faculty of JIIT along with the leaders from the industry to lay the stone of entrepreneurship opportunities.

Missions and Goals Of JIIC

JIIC works on various missions such as:

  • It generates Intellectual Property Rights(IPR).
  • It motivates students to think innovative that is out of the box.
  • It ensures the setting up of institutional innovative research goals.
  • It encourages interdisciplinary research.
  • It promotes participative innovative leadership.
  • It becomes the catalyst for a step change in the JIIT's innovation capability.

There are various goals of JIIC such as to:

  • Provide facilities and high end infrastructure Technology,focused Incubator where innovation consulting and other research oriented opportunities can grow.
  • Share and exchange of ideas, thoughts and knowledge,there by making a healthy ecosystem for entrepreneurs, startups and provides opportunity to new innovative ideas.
  • Create opportunities for the new innovative and startup oriented ideas in JIIT.

About JIIT

Jaypee Institute of information technology is one of the leading institutions in the field of science and business. The institute serves as a stepping stone for students from all over the country. The main campus, situated in one of the major cities of india- Noida, sector 62 boasts it's modern infrastructure, top notch amenities and a lush green campus. The institute provides undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various fields of study ranging from science and technology to business. The students are provided with the most prestigious opportunities to pivot their careers in the right direction. The faculty at JIIT believes in the absolute potential of each and every individual and is willing to put in the hours for the future of their students. The institute has been graced with some of the best teachers in their respective fields who make every learning experience better. While being a top institution for science and tech, JIIT also provides a solid environment for students with entrepreneurial endeavors. This is facilitated by the numerous societies and clubs that are a part of the ecosystem at JIIT. These clubs act as creative, academic and entrepreneurial outlets for students where they can showcase their true potential in amalgamation with their intellectual abilities. Here at JIIT, students are encouraged to pursue their passions, to grasp the opportunities that come their way and to make the best of them.
